About Hamid Financials

Your trusted partner in financial success with years of experience and unwavering commitment to excellence.



Our Mission

Our mission is to deliver high quality Tax, Advisory, and accounting services to individuals and small to mid-sized businesses. We strive to build lasting relationships with our clients by providing personalized attention and expert guidance.

Our Vision

At Hamid Financials, we envision financial clarity being a tool to empower individuals and businesses to achieve their fullest potential. We strive to be the beacon of trust and expertise in the financial landscape, guiding our clients towards prosperity with innovative solutions and unwavering integrity.

Meet Our Team

Our team of dedicated professionals brings together decades of experience in tax, accounting, and financial services.

Ihsan Hamid, CPA

Managing Partner

Ihsan Hamid is a distinguished graduate of the University of California Riverside, holding a Bachelor of Science degree in Business Accounting. As a CPA, he is a dedicated member of both the AICPA and CalCPA. Ihsan's career began at Novogradac & Company, LLP, where he specialized in tax and audit services, focusing on LIHTC and new markets tax industries. He later transitioned to RSM US LLP, gaining expertise in international tax consulting. As the managing partner of Hamid Financials LLP, Ihsan is dedicated to building strong client relationships and maintaining excellence in every interaction.

Irfan Hamid

Marketing Specialist

With an educational background in Cognitive Science with specialization in User Experience Design at UC San Diego and valuable experience as a student leader, and business professional, Irfan has and continues to make notable accomplishments in every environment and organization he steps foot into. Irfan serves as a strategic advisor on the leadership team, using his prowess to kickstart movements unthought of.

Kamal Hamid

Staff Accountant

Kamal Hamid is a Tax Staff Accountant with expertise in tax preparation and financial analysis. He is a graduate of the University of California, Riverside, with a concentration in Accounting. Kamal plays a key role in preparing and reviewing tax returns for individuals, partnerships, and corporations, and is committed to delivering high-quality services to clients.
