Meet Our Team

Our team of dedicated professionals brings together decades of experience in tax, accounting, and financial services.

Ihsan Hamid, CPA

Managing Partner

Ihsan Hamid is a distinguished graduate of the University of California Riverside, holding a Bachelor of Science degree in Business Accounting. As a CPA, he is a dedicated member of both the AICPA and CalCPA. Ihsan's career began at Novogradac & Company, LLP, where he specialized in tax and audit services, focusing on LIHTC and new markets tax industries. He later transitioned to RSM US LLP, gaining expertise in international tax consulting. As the managing partner of Hamid Financials LLP, Ihsan is dedicated to building strong client relationships and maintaining excellence in every interaction.
