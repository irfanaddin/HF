About Us

Our Vision

Our vision is to instill complete confidence in the services we provide to our clients who have chosen us as their business partners. Everything we do is rooted in this vision and supported by six core values that guide how we work with each other and how we serve our clients.

Our Core Values

  • Integrity - Listen and understand, be honest and transparent, and be direct.
  • Excellence - Be curious, seek better ways, and stay vigilant.
  • Togetherness - Embrace a 'no one left behind' mentality.
  • Accountability - Follow through on your word, be humble, and take personal responsibility.
  • Agile and Lean - Be forward-thinking and ready, willing, and able to act quickly on changes.
  • Knowledge - Invest in yourself, share your knowledge, and have the courage to point out deficiencies constructively.

Meet Our Team

Our team comprises dedicated professionals committed to delivering exceptional financial services with integrity and excellence.

Ihsan Hamid, CPA - Managing Partner

Ihsan Hamid is a distinguished graduate of the University of California Riverside, holding a Bachelor of Science degree in Business Accounting. As a CPA, he is a dedicated member of both the AICPA and CalCPA. Ihsan's career began at Novogradac & Company, LLP, where he specialized in tax and audit services, focusing on LIHTC and new markets tax industries. He later transitioned to RSM US LLP, gaining expertise in international tax consulting. As the managing partner of Hamid Financials LLP, Ihsan is dedicated to building strong client relationships and maintaining excellence in every interaction.

Imran Hamid - Manager

Imran Hamid is a Tax Staff Accountant with a background in accounting and tax preparation. He holds a bachelor’s degree in Accounting from the University of California, Riverside, and is pursuing his Master’s in Tax at California State University, Fullerton. Imran's responsibilities include preparing and reviewing tax returns, maintaining financials, and ensuring clear communication with clients. He is recognized for his skills in tax preparation, financial analysis, and client communication.
