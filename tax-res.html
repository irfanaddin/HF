Resolution Tools We Use

Our expert team can guide you through various tax resolution strategies, customized to your needs

Installment Agreement

Set up a payment plan with the IRS to pay off your tax debt over time.

Offer In Compromise (OIC)

Negotiate a settlement that allows you to pay less than the full amount owed.

Innocent Spouse Relief

Get relief from tax liabilities caused by your spouse's or former spouse's errors.

Penalty Abatement

Request a reduction or removal of penalties for failure to file or pay on time, based on reasonable cause.

Currently Not Collectible (CNC) Status

If you can't pay due to financial hardship, we can help you get your account marked as uncollectible.
